Jacob's World Famous Andouille is located on U.S. 61 just West of the start of  U.S. Hwy 51.  We are about a 20 minute drive from the New Orleans International Airport and about 35 minutes from the CBD.  Our address is 505 West Airline Hwy in LaPlace, LA.   We are only 3.3 miles off of I-10, so when you drive through Southeast Louisiana, drop off the interstate at Exit 209 for a little break and come visit us.  You are welcome to take a free tour of our smokehouse facility.

We have included a map on this page with driving directions for your convience.  Or, you can click on the map for a printable page with the map and driving directions on it.  Then use your browser's back button to return.

Driving West from New Orleans, cross the 12 mile bridge on I-10 and take the first LaPlace Exit (Exit 209).  Driving East from Baton Rouge, take the second LaPlace Exit (Exit 209). Turn South on U.S. Hwy 51 and drive about 3.3 miles to U.S. 61 (Airline Hwy.) Take a right turn onto U.S. 61 going West and we are about 400 feet on the left side of the Airline Hwy in the little barn shaped building.
